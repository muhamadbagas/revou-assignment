About Us


Welcome to the RevoU International School! We are a premiere education institution that offers world-class education in the field of Information Technology. Our school is dedicated to providing students with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in the dynamic and rapidly-evolving IT industry.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision:

Our goal is to be a leading IT community school in Indonesia and providing students to become successful professional developers in the technology industry.

Mission:

Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible, and affordable IT education to learners of all backgrounds and skill levels. We serve our students with the knowledge, skills, and tools they need to succeed in the technology industry.
